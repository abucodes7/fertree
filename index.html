Welcome To FERTREE

“Smart Farming, Thriving Trees.”

FERTREE is an intelligent, battery-powered fertilizing machine designed to automate soil enrichment and grass maintenance for large-scale tree plantations, ensuring efficiency and sustainability.

About

Ensuring sustainable and efficient tree plantation management requires optimizing fertilization and maintenance through smart automation. Developing advanced fertilization methods serves the dual purpose of enhancing tree growth and reducing fertilizer wastage without precise nutrient assessment. Along with the development of better plantation techniques, efficient fertilization is thus a paramount goal for maximizing tree health and productivity. The traditional method of fertilization and grass maintenance has been manual labor by farmers, which is time-consuming and costly, making it infeasible for large-scale and small to medium-sized plantations worldwide.

Features

Automated Fertilization Arm

Precisely digs, dispenses, and covers fertilizer to ensure optimal nutrient absorption.

360-Degree Grass Cutting System

Maintains a clear area around trees, eliminating the need for herbicides.

Battery-Powered & Portable Design

Ensures eco-friendly operation with easy mobility across plantations.
